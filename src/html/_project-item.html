Lucidica

Lucidica is an IT company helping UK small and medium-sized businesses with technical challenges, innovations, and data protection. We redesigned their website to improve online presence, clarify services, and ensure a modern look.

Design Process

Research & Analysis

Before designing the new website, we conducted a detailed competitor analysis and gathered user feedback. The main issues of the previous design included:

  • - Complicated navigation – users struggled to find the services they needed.
  • - No mobile responsiveness – the site did not function properly on smartphones.
  • - Outdated UI – the visual style no longer met modern standards.

Wireframes & Prototyping

Before moving to the final design, we created wireframes to structure the layout and define key user flows. This step ensured an intuitive and efficient user experience by focusing on functionality before aesthetics:

  • - Low-Fidelity Wireframes – Early sketches to map out the structure and user interactions.
  • - High-Fidelity Wireframes – Detailed layouts incorporating key UI elements.
  • - Interactive Prototypes – Clickable mockups to test usability and gather feedback before development.

By refining the design through wireframing and prototyping, we minimized revisions and ensured a seamless transition from concept to a fully functional product.

UI KIT Design

To ensure consistency and scalability, I developed a comprehensive UI Kit that serves as the foundation for the entire design system. It includes essential components, typography, colors, buttons, icons, and form elements, ensuring a cohesive visual language across all pages:

  • - Typography & Color Palette – Defines the brand’s tone and accessibility.
  • - Buttons & Forms – Designed for usability and responsiveness.
  • - Icons & Components – Ensuring clarity and consistency.
  • - Spacing & Grid System – Optimized for a seamless user experience.

The UI Kit streamlines the design process, improves collaboration, and maintains a unified aesthetic throughout the project.

Design Process

Main page

The homepage immediately communicates the company’s expertise, showcasing Lucidica's strong technical knowledge and commitment to innovation. We utilized bold visuals and modern design elements to reflect their innovative approach. The layout is user-friendly, making it easy for visitors to navigate and understand the services offered. Additionally, we focused on a clean and professional aesthetic that aligns with the company's identity, ensuring a seamless experience for users across all devices.

Services Page

The Services page is designed to clearly showcase the range of services offered by the company. It highlights the company's expertise in each service category, ensuring that visitors can easily understand what is available. Bold typography and concise descriptions are used to guide the user through the offerings, with each service section accompanied by intuitive icons and visuals to enhance understanding. The layout is organized to allow quick access to key information, and the design is responsive, ensuring a smooth browsing experience on any device. The page maintains a professional yet approachable aesthetic, reinforcing the company's credibility while making it easy for users to explore and find solutions tailored to their needs.

About Us Page

The About Us page is designed to provide visitors with a clear understanding of the company’s values, mission, and history. We aimed to create a connection by highlighting the team’s expertise and commitment to delivering innovative solutions. The design incorporates engaging visuals, including team photos and key milestones, to humanize the brand and convey the company’s story. The layout is structured to lead users through the company’s journey, from its founding to its present-day achievements. With a focus on transparency and trust, the page creates a warm and professional atmosphere that encourages visitors to learn more about the company’s culture and vision, while ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.

Conclusions

The redesign delivered measurable results, demonstrating its significant value for the client:

Increased Website Traffic: A 40% increase in traffic, driven by improved user experience and SEO, led to longer visits and deeper engagement across the site.

Higher Conversion Rates: A 25% boost in conversions, with visitors more easily navigating the site and engaging with services, resulting in more inquiries.

Enhanced User Engagement: User-friendly features and a visually appealing design led to better engagement, with positive feedback on ease of use and overall experience.

Stronger Brand Identity: The new design reinforced the client’s innovative, professional image, boosting brand recognition and positioning them as an industry leader.

Improved Mobile Experience: Mobile traffic increased by 30%, thanks to a responsive design that ensures a smooth experience on all devices.
